Vision

Ethiopian Tour Operator Association

The vision for Ethiopian Tour Operator Association is global, attracting People and influencers in business. To contribute resources through time or funding to the growth and development of business. It also supports mentors in establishing their communities within the countries or towns that they live in.

Mission
